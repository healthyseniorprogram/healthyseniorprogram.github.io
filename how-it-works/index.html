How It Works

  1. Blue Cross’ virtual reality partner, AppliedVR, will mail your virtual reality headset to you and schedule time for your onboarding session.
  2. Once you are comfortable with using the device, you’ll then be carefully guided through a series of virtual reality programs that will take 6 to 15 minutes daily for the following 8 weeks.
  3. You’ll also be encouraged to participate in small virtual social groups that can be done from the privacy of your home and will be held for about 25-30 minutes once a week.
  4. You will also be asked to complete brief surveys to share your experience in the program
  5. At the conclusion of the study, you’ll use shipping materials and instructions for sending the device back that were provided to you at the study start.
