How It Works

Setting Up Your Garmin Vivofit® Account

Download Garmin Connect from the Apple Store for iPhones or Google Play Store for Android phones. Open and select Create Account.

Accept the legal notifications and then click Next.

Update relevant account information and then click Next.

Follow on-screen instructions to pair your Garmin Vivofit 4 device.

Downloading the MoveSpring® Application

Download the MoveSpring app from the Apple Store for iPhones or Google Play Store for Androids.

Open the application and select that you are new to MoveSpring.

Insert HEALTHY as the organization code and select Sign Up. Confirm the Organization and accept the terms of the waiver.

Complete additional questions.

Setting Up a MoveSpring Personal Account

Create your account. Select a fun user name and image that will be displayed throughout the application. To be anonymous, do not use your actual name or picture in the app.

Connect your device. Select Garmin and "Allow" MoveSpring to access your data to help with tracking.

Once re-directed to the Garmin website, insert your Garmin Account information (the email and password used to set up your Garmin device).

Optional: Complete your Profile.
