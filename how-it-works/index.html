How It Works

Setting Up Your Garmin Vivofit® Account

Download Garmin Connect from the Apple Store or Google Play for Android Phones. Open and select Create Account.

Accept the legal notifications and then click Next.

Update relevant account information and then click Next.

Accept the legal notifications and then click Next.

Downloading the MoveSpring® Application

Search for MoveSpring in the Apple Store or in Google Play for Android phones and download the application.

Open the application and select that you are new to MoveSpring.

Insert HEALTHY as the organization code. Hit Confirm on the next screen.

Optional: Complete info questions.

Setting Up a MoveSpring Personal Account

Create account.

Click Garmin and "Allow" MoveSpring to access your data to help with tracking.

Once re-directed to the Garmin website, insert your Garmin Account information (the email and password used to set up the Garmin).

Optional: Complete your profile.
