We're thrilled to invite you to participate in Blue Cross Blue Shield of Michigan's Virtual Reality for Healthy Seniors program.

This no-cost, eight-week program is available exclusively to a select group of Blue Cross members, including you. It's a new program designed to help you become better equipped to manage your pain and to become more socially connected with others, even as you continue social distancing. All you need is:

  • A smartphone and a computer with internet to complete the web-based surveys
  • Wireless internet access at home
  • A willingness to participate in weekly group sessions
  • A commitment to spend a few minutes with the headset each day

Healthy Senior Program Features and Benefits

Pico virtual reality headset, powered by Blue Cross' virtual reality partner, AppliedVR, available to you for the entire eight-week program.

Proven, guided VR pain management applications, designed and validated by physicians, medical researchers and other experts.

Virtual social support groups, hosted by a moderator and attended by up to 10 others, so you can enjoy connecting with other seniors in Michigan from the safety of your home.

Weekly emails to help you track your progress through the guided VR programs.

Unfortunately due to high demand, this program's enrollment is full. At Blue Cross, we're always looking for ways to help you better your health. While this program is full, there may be other opportunities in the future.

You can stay in touch with us through our website or by calling the number on the back of your Blue Cross member ID card.
