We're thrilled to invite you to participate in Blue Cross Blue Shield of Michigan's Virtual Reality for Healthy Seniors Study!

This no-cost, 8-week study is available exclusively to a small group of Blue Cross members, including you! It’s a new benefit designed to help you become better equipped to manage your pain and isolation, and to become more socially connected with others, even as you continue social distancing.

All you need is a smartphone or other device with speaker and video capabilities, home WiFi, and a willingness to participate in weekly group sessions and spend a few minutes daily during the 8 weeks with the headset.

Healthy Senior Program Features

Pico virtual reality headset, powered by Blue Cross Blue Shield of Michigan's virtual reality partner, AppliedVR, available to you for the entire 8-week study.

Proven, guided virtual reality pain management applications, designed and validated by physicians, medical researchers, and other experts.

Virtual social support groups, hosted by a moderator and attended by up to 10 others, so you can enjoy connecting with other seniors in Michigan from the safety of your home.

Weekly emails to help you track your progress through the guided VR programs.

There are only 100 spots available. Don’t miss out – sign up today for a chance to participate!
