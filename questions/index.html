Questions?

What is the Healthy Senior Pilot?

Healthy Senior is a pilot (one-time test program) offered by Blue Cross Blue Shield of Michigan. It is a unique pilot program offered to selected Blue Cross Medicare Advantage members. This beneficial program offers a free Garmin Vívofit fitness tracker (which is yours to keep) and access to a step-tracking and fitness challenge application (app) called MoveSpring. The MoveSpring app has an associated "Talk" feature where you can connect with a Healthy Senior representatives about anything on your mind.

Benefits of the pilot program:

  • No cost to members
  • No effect on rates or future claims
  • Free wearable device to track activity, and access to a Healthy Senior representative to discuss anything

Why are you giving me a free wearable Garmin Vívofit?

Blue Cross Blue Shield of Michigan is always looking for innovative ways to provide better service to its members. This pilot is one of the many ways that Blue Cross is trying to improve interactions with our members. The Garmin Vívofit is part of the Healthy Senior Pilot program. If you choose to participate in this pilot program, the Garmin is yours to keep.

Are there any downsides of participating in the Healthy Senior Pilot program?

No. The Healthy Senior program has no penalties, no fees, and no effect on your rates or future claims. The pilot program is completely optional. You can stop participating at any time. The Garmin is yours to keep.

How long does the pilot last?

The Healthy Senior Pilot program is a four-month program, starting July 1, 2018, and going through October 31, 2018. The Garmin is yours to keep.

Will my information be kept private?

All information is stored in a highly secured manner. The security of your information is of utmost importance to Blue Cross.

How was I selected?

A small group with the most potential to benefit was selected for the Healthy Senior pilot program. We hope you choose to enroll!

How do I enroll in the Healthy Senior pilot program?

If you are selected, a Healthy Senior pilot representative will reach out to you over the phone to begin the enrollment process. You will also receive information about the Healthy Senior pilot program in the mail.

Only members selected for this pilot program can enroll at this time.

What does the Garmin Vívofit do?

The Garmin Vívofit is a fitness band that tracks steps, distance, calories, and sleep. It can send beeps to remind you to move during periods of prolonged sitting. You can see tracked information on the MoveSpring app and you can use this information to help you live a healthier lifestyle.

How do I set up the device?

We will mail detailed instructions about how to set up the device. If you have not received the instructions, please click here to see the detailed instructions.

Who can I contact for help setting up the Garmin Vívofit?

For any Garmin device related issues, please reach out to their customer service by visiting https://support.garmin.com/en-US/ or call them at 1-800-800-1020.

What if I want to sync a different device with the MoveSpring app?

If you already have a wearable device that you would like to use instead of the Garmin Vívofit, please feel free to do so. Set up a MoveSpring account as directed, then sync your device instead of the Garmin when instructed.

What is the MoveSpring app?

The MoveSpring app is a fun, easy-to-use health app that can sync with the Garmin Vívofit (or another fitness tracker) and help you see your tracked information over a longer period of time. You can also track your mood over time and make "Talk" requests.

How do I download the MoveSpring app?

Detailed instructions on how to download and set up the MoveSpring app will be mailed to you. If you have not received the instructions in the mail, please go to the Apple Store for iPhones or Google Play Store for Androids and search for "MoveSpring" to download the application. Follow the instructions in the app to finish setting it up.

How do I use the MoveSpring app?

Download the MoveSpring app on your phone, and login to have access to ‘Call-Me’ requests and check your steps tracked from the Garmin. You can also track your progress against goals you set for yourself in the app.

Does the MoveSpring app work on my phone?

The MoveSpring app will only work on a smartphone (iOS/ Android) with the most up-to-date software.

Can I still participate if I don't have a smartphone?

You can still participate and set up the Garmin even if you don't have access to a smartphone. The MoveSpring app can be set up on the website: https://app.movespring.com/.

The Garmin will work with partial functionality (the step count will work automatically) prior to any linking.

Who is going to be calling me?

One of our Healthy Senior Pilot representatives will reaching out to you to address your needs. They will listen to anything you have to say. Remember - if you have a medical emergency, call 911 immediately.

What is the "Talk" feature?

If you use the "Talk" feature, one of our Healthy Senior representatives will call you to discuss anything on your mind. If you have a medical emergency, please call 911 immediately. If you have a question about plan benefits, please contact Medicare Plus Blue PPO Customer Service at 1-855-237-3501.

When is the "Talk" feature available? What if I request a "Talk" outside of operating hours?

Our Healthy Senior will be ready to call you between 9 a.m. and 5 p.m.,Eastern time, Monday through Friday. If you request a call outside of these hours, a representative will call you the following business day after 10 a.m., Eastern time.
